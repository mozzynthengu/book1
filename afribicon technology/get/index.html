Afribicon IT Solutions – Site Plan

Empowering Malawi Through Technology

Site Plan for WDD 131 Final Project

1. Purpose

The purpose of this IT Services website is to showcase Afribicon’s digital solutions—including cybersecurity, networking, development, and IT support— designed to empower organizations in Malawi. The website provides clear service information, company values, and easy navigation for users seeking reliable technology assistance.

2. Audience

Our target audience includes small businesses, NGOs, corporations, educational institutions, and individuals in Malawi who need secure, affordable, and professional IT services. This site is built for users who may not be highly tech-savvy and need simple explanations and trustworthy service.

3. Dynamic Elements (JavaScript)

  • A service category filter to sort services (using arrays + filter()).
  • A rotating testimonial slider (array + DOM updates + timers).
  • A dark mode toggle saved using localStorage.
  • A contact form validator that provides helpful user feedback.

4. Logo

The logo will feature a clean, modern tech icon such as a shield, gear, or circuit design with the initials “A” or “Afribicon”. (Logo will be placed on the home page.)

5. Colors

  • Primary Blue: #0052cc
  • Secondary Navy: #0d1b2a
  • Accent Green: #00c853
  • Light Neutral: #f4f4f4
  • Dark Neutral: #1a1a1a

6. Fonts

Headings: Montserrat
Body Text: Roboto
