Overview

Purpose

AFRIBICON TECHNOLOGY provides professional digital solutions including website design, hosting, custom systems, branding, IT consulting, and cybersecurity. The purpose of the site is to present our services, display our portfolio, share pricing packages, and allow customers to contact us for quotes and support.

Audience

Our audience includes small and medium businesses, schools, clinics, NGOs, entrepreneurs, and individuals seeking modern, affordable digital solutions. The site is designed for users who want professional technology services such as web development, branding, and IT consulting.

Dynamic Elements

JavaScript will be used for interactive and dynamic features including:

  • Service filtering using arrays and filter().
  • Dark/light mode toggle (DOM manipulation + conditionals).
  • Expandable service descriptions using event listeners.
  • “Saved services” using objects and localStorage.
  • Modules: services.js (data), ui.js (display), main.js (logic).
